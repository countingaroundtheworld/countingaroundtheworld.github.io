Did you know?

Numbers are counted differently in parts of the world. This is because of the language spoken and the writing style used are not the same in most of the countries. There are Arabic, Chinese, English, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Spanish and hundreds more that we could learn.

This website is dedicated to show how numbers are counted in different languages. Lets start by choosing one of the activities in the menu above.
